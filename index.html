Welcome 💖

In the spring of 2025, I joined Sand Rarity as a sales assistant intern in their pop-up store located in Galeries Lafayette Reims. Over two months, under the guidance of Sandy BOGET, I immersed myself in luxury customer service, product knowledge, and experiential selling. This experience taught me how to communicate effectively, develop product passion, and work as part of a boutique team.

About Sand Rarity ✨

Sand Rarity is a French premium skincare brand born from the desire to combine natural science and luxury elegance. The brand specializes in products made with organic prickly pear seed oil — known for its antioxidant richness and rarity.

  • 🌿 99% natural ingredients, Ecocert certified
  • 🏔️ Handcrafted in the French Alps
  • 💎 Focused on holistic beauty, sustainability, and innovation

The brand's philosophy embraces eco-luxury — offering environmentally conscious skincare that respects skin health while delivering refined textures and scents. Sand Rarity targets women who seek authenticity, transparency, and sensual beauty rituals.

Critical Thinking 🧠

Prior to my internship, I believed that start-ups were chaotic and lacked structure. Sand Rarity proved me wrong. I discovered a brand that, while agile and adaptive, relied on clear processes, team rituals, and strong values. Each action — from customer greeting to stock check — had its importance in delivering excellence.

What I Learned vs. What I Expected

  • Expected: Autonomy without guidance
    Observed: Empowerment with coaching and feedback
  • Expected: Fast and messy
    Observed: Fast but thoughtful
  • Expected: Purely business
    Observed: Emotionally engaging mission and customer connection

Advantages

  • 🌟 Real-time responsibility and impact
  • 💬 Constant communication and feedback loops
  • 🤍 Emotional engagement with brand values

Limitations

  • ⏱️ High rhythm can be overwhelming at first
  • 📚 Training is mostly informal and experiential
